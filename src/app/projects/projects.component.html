SLDB

SLDB(Service Life Database) is a website I built as a commision with a friend and partner of mine @Femi Black for the purpose of storing and retrieving data on several buildings that have undergone specific maintenance and checks.
There were a few hurdles to jump through as the data was many and varied and neither of us had worked with such amounts of data before.
The project was completed successfully and two versions of it ended up being developed, one in Vue(@Femi Black) and one in Angular(Me) with the backend for both developed by @Femi Black
